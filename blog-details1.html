Long Distance

By Praneeta
  • Home
  • Blog Listing
  • Blog Details

He wants to see her lips beam with a smile that he puts on her face He wants to see the twinkle in her eyes as she laughs to his jokes He wants to see the blush lighting up her cheeks when he tells her how beautiful she is He wants to see her face as she is falling asleep in front of him and not through the laptop He wants to be the only one to kiss her goodnight He wants to hold her when she is crying telling her " its okay, i am here now" he wants to look at all the faces she would make to annoy him and grin at her to make her more angry

He wants to punch all the faces who try to snuggle upto her He wants to be her prince He wants to hold her in the star lit sky and tell her , her eyes are more beautiful than the stars He wants it all He wipes his tears away as he is staring at her picture He is a million miles away but his thoughts are right where she is She is the reason for his smile, for every breathe he takes She is his courage She is his muse

And when one day they would meet each he would hold her in his arms and tell her "Though without you life has been hard , seeing you everything feels easy and i could do it all over, just to see you and hold you like this again"
