Love

By Praneeta

Most of us are callous enough to name infatuation as "Love" love isnt flirting, sex or hugs as our mainstream cinema potrays it to be. when what is love ? love is about taking all these things away and still having the feeling for that person. just stop yourself for a moment and think about what the feeling could be instead of just googling or narrating random prose from the greatest love sagas

for me, LOVE is when the most disinteresting qualites about you become a source of amazement for each others eyes Love is when someone cant stop looking at your facial expression of yours and smile to themselves. love is when somone thinks you are the most beautiful person even in the morning when you wake up with tousled eyes and unkempt hair love is when someone reveals with all glory on the concept of "US" and thinks with a smile on the face on how the kids would be irritating as you . Love is when someone earns a dime a day but spends it wholeheartedly to see you sleep serenely content at night. love is when someone gives up their big dreams to create an even smaller shared dream with you

love is when you feel complete while holding shaky hands and look into those wrinkled lines on their faces without ever feeling tired at the sunset years of your life love is when you can have a heated argument for hours and suddenly burst out laughing for no apparent reason love is when someone counts every moment spent with you to be precious, life changing and sacred that is simply irreplaceable love is this and many more
