My heart raced fast almost skipped a beat when i saw what was in front of me As i walked down the aisle, There you were standing perfectly still with a perfect smile on your face. As i took a step forward I knew i took a step towards eternal love In my hand was a white piece of paper which had everything i wanted to tell you my vows.

As i came closer, you reached for my hand and held it ,like you tried to say "everything is gonna be fine" your eyes gleemed with joy you stood still with a flawless smile on your face i wondered what was going on in your mind. Just then the cermony began and i couldnt help but wonder if it was all just a dream. That very moment you pulled out a ring i could hear my heartbeat race faster i've been with you for many years and yet i stood to admire you and before i could thing any further you said "Before i met you,i thought my world had everything i needed to be happy.Then you walked into my life and everything changed.I realised how empty my world was without you in it .I Love everything about you. I love the way you look at me like no one ever has, and i love the way you love me like no one ever has. I cant imagine my life without you and if you say "yes" to me in few minutes i dont have to"

As i said "yes" i couldnt help but to tear up. At that very moment i realised i dint need a "speech" to tell how i felt about him i tried to write our fairytale, and tried to fix it with some fancy words I took a deep breath, and said how i truly felt "Before i met you my world had nothing,You've taught me to trust, how to let someone in and what it truly means to fall in love. I cant possibly describe how much i love you, so ill tell you why i love you. You see the world in a way that no one else does and you appreciate everything including me. There's no one in the world like you , and if you say "yes"in a few minutes, i'll get to spend the rest of my life trying to see the world through your eyes,appreciating everything including you...the wonderful certainly the most handsome man i have ever met" As he said a "yes i do" i could hear nothing more. i just realised i would be spending the rest of my life with the love of my life
