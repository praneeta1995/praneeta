What is Love?

By Praneeta
  • Home
  • Blog Listing
  • Blog Details

“I am in love” A phrase we all get to hear from almost every teenager every now and then When i came across that phrase for the very first time, I had many questions on my mind, “Is love the same FOREVER ?”, “does it really exist ?”, “do people really mean it ?” Saying those three magical words is so easy, all it takes is a little bit of courage, some sweet words and one grand gesture. But keeping that love alive takes some serious work. so how do I define love ? .

Love is not an illusion , love is not a want, love is a need. When you find the right person love is special. You are ready to sacrifice anything and everything to let that someone know how much you care for them love is compassion . love is kind. Love is him . That one person who can change your world , that one person who can be your support system, who can guide you , can scold you . love is blind , the one you like might not be the most handsome or the most beautiful one in this world but is certainly the most beautiful one in your world. Love is trust,

love is not the money.Everyone loves to be pampered but it shouldn’t be the sole reason why you are in a relationship. Money can buy any kind of happiness, but it cannot buy love or trust of a person. Love is often confused with lust , its okay to be into him but a relationship shouldn’t be based on it, when love becomes lust , feelings get messed up and things get ugly. Love is you When you love someone, that one person’s feelings matter the most to you, it doesn’t matter who is with you or who isn’t as long as that person is with you. For me he is everything my world needs My prince, my soulmate.
