When Love arrived

By Praneeta

When I was 15 I knew exactly how love would look like when it arrived Love was a little shorter than I was He was an year older Love made me feel good He taught me the little things in life But love dint last long Love faded away as quickly as it arrived I dint know what went wrong But I knew I had to move on.

Years later Love arrived again This time I thought I knew love was here to stay He was dorky ,slient , sweet He would have a cute smile on his face from time to time Love showed me it's okay to laugh Love showed me how to be myself Just When I was just getting comfortable Love disappeared Maybe I was not ready for love Maybe love was not ready for me Maybe love chose to live in a different timezone as I did Love happens when it is suppose to And love leaves when it's time I thought love would not happen again

But right then love arrived I dint know if I should trust love this time Love was super tall Love smelt different now He had freckles and a cute smile that I barely recognised Love had songs that reminds me of him His irregular sleeping patterns that I need to get used to We would find a bench on the park that would perfect fit us We would hold hands and know we never want to let go Love would say "you are beautiful " time to time and mean it Love would says " you are beautiful" even when I would cry Love liked me the way I was Love makes me wanna look at the stars and just feel the air I know now that love isn't gonna dissapear If it is not forever It's not love
